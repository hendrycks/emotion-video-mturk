Emotional Responses to Videos

Consent to Participate in Research
    By accepting this hit, you are participating in a study being performed by researchers Alan Cowen & Dacher Keltner in the Department of Psychology at the University of California, Berkeley. You must be 18+ to participate.
   This study concerns the different emotions that can be elicited by videos. Your participation in this research is voluntary. You have a right to withdraw at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. Though measures will be taken to protect your confidentiality, there is still a risk that your confidentiality could be compromised. To minimize this risk, we will disassociate your WorkerID from your responses after they have been downloaded. We will not receive any other personally identifying information about you. When the research is completed, we may save the data indefinitely for use in future research. The data will be password-protected at all times and accessible only to the researchers named above.
    Compensation for this survey is $0.72. There are no other personal benefits, except potential enjoyment of amusing or interesting videos. However, other videos may be highly unpleasant to watch. It is hoped that this research will contribute to our understanding of how to cultivate positive emotions and reduce negative ones.
    If you have questions about this research, please contact Alan Cowen at alan.cowen@berkeley.edu. For further questions about your rights as a research participant, please contact the University of California at Berkeley's Committee for Protection of Human Subjects at 510-642-7461 or subjects@berkeley.edu.
 

In this study, you will see 30 videos. Alongside each video will be a search menu. You will use the search menu to choose one or more emotions that capture how each video makes you feel. We encourage you to complete as many of these HITs as you like. At the beginning of the first HIT only, you will be required to answer a few demographic and personality questions.

WARNING: Videos in this HIT may contain real footage of graphic violence, nudity, and/or sexual content. Please exercise discretion. Do not risk exposing others to graphic imagery. You must be 18+ to view this HIT.

Instructions

Select the emotions that describe how each video below makes you feel.

  • A different video will appear as you hover each row of this survey. (If the videos fail to appear, do not accept this hit.) Each video will loop continuously, one at a time. Please watch each video for at least one loop before responding to it.

  • If you choose randomly you may be rejected.
  • After you have selected at least one emotion for each video in this HIT, click the continue button at the bottom of the page.
